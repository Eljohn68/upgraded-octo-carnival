About Us

Colegio de San Uriel is a values-centered academic institution in Cebu. Inspired by St. Uriel, we form learners who pursue truth, virtue, and service to community.

Our Mission

The mission of Colegio de San Uriel is to educate and form students who are intellectually competent, morally upright, spiritually grounded, and socially responsible through quality education and values-based formation.

Our Vision

Colegio de San Uriel envisions a community of learners guided by faith, wisdom, and integrity, forming morally upright, academically excellent, and socially responsible individuals who serve God and society.

Why Choose Colegio de San Uriel

Choosing the right school is one of the most important decisions a student and family can make. It is not only about academics, but about finding a place where character is shaped, values are strengthened, and potential is discovered. Colegio de San Uriel stands as a school that offers more than education—it offers formation for life.

Colegio de San Uriel is founded on the belief that true excellence is achieved when knowledge is guided by virtue. Inspired by the wisdom and courage of St. Uriel, the institution is committed to forming students who are intellectually competent, morally upright, spiritually grounded, and socially responsible. Every lesson, activity, and interaction is designed to nurture not only the mind, but also the heart and soul of each learner.

What sets Colegio de San Uriel apart is its holistic approach to education. Academic excellence is balanced with values-based formation, ensuring that students grow into responsible individuals who can think critically, act ethically, and serve compassionately. Teachers are not only instructors, but mentors who guide students with dedication, care, and integrity. The school environment promotes discipline, respect, and a strong sense of community, where every learner is valued and supported.

Beyond the classroom, Colegio de San Uriel encourages students to become active contributors to society. Through community involvement, leadership opportunities, and faith-centered activities, learners are prepared to face real-world challenges with confidence and humility. The school aims to produce graduates who are not only successful in their chosen paths, but who also use their knowledge and talents in service to God and others.

To join Colegio de San Uriel is to become part of a growing community united by faith, wisdom, and excellence. It is a place where students are inspired to dream, guided to achieve, and formed to lead with integrity. For those seeking an education that shapes both character and competence, Colegio de San Uriel is not just a school—it is a home for lifelong learning and purposeful living.
